Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

The Casa Milan Store, located at the iconic Rossoneri headquarters in Milan's dynamic Portello district, offers a wide selection of official AC Milan merchandise. You can find Match Kits from the ongoing season, casual clothing, numerous accessories, gift ideas and so much more to make sure you're always sporting Rossoneri colours and representing the passion and elegance that define our Club.

There is also an express shirt printing service available in store. Select your preferred name and number and have your personalised shirt ready in minutes.

Our sales team will be ready to support your every need, giving you the best advice on any purchase queries you may have. And if you're unsure of what gift to buy, you can also purchase a gift card valid for any in-store or online purchases at store.acmilan.com

The store is open every day of the week from Monday to Sunday with daily opening hours of 10:00 to 18:00.
